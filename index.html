About Crossroads

Well, the invitation to pursue that flighty temptress – adventure is still open, this time in the virtual world.Round every corner, a challenge will lie in wait, beyond every click of the mouse will lurk a new dare. Every Dragon and Troll will reveal an unexpected twist in its tail.

Each contest will test your true mettlem, from plotting maps to cracking spells! The Wizarding World of Harry Potter continues to entice one and all, so pick up the gauntlet and join this clash of the wizards! Dig out your wands, shake out your cloaks and polish up your spells because the magic is soon to be unleashed!

Platform 9 3⁄4

So, step onto Platform 9¾ for an action- packed ride on the Hogwarts Express in Round I. Here you’ll journey across the mystic Indian land in search of an elusive Horcrux, test your alchemical skills, explore the mysteries of a magical vault and craftily outwit your opponents.

Diagon Alley

Get off the train at Diagon Alley to explore the goodies in Round II. Grab your copy of The Daily Prophet to test your language skills, battle your beastly opponents with your amazing dance moves and sing your Potter ballad to beat the competition.

Triwizard Tournament

And for those who stay ahead of the pack, welcome to the grand finale where the Wizard of Wow will clash in Round III – Triwizard Tournament. This is the ultimate test of your wizarding powers.
